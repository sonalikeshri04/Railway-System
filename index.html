SYSTEM Services

Indian Railway is the backbone of the country's logistics sector. We carry more than 1.2 Billon tonnes of the freight traffic every year over a network of 68000 kms. With our network touching almost every nook and corner of the country, we play a crucical role in facilitating a balanced and inclusive socio economics develoment of the country. We carry almost all commodities including bulk commodities like coal, iron ore , iron & steel, Food grains, cement, petroleum products, fertilizers and other commodities carried in containers. We are proud to serve more than 9,000 customers. we are the most environment friendly mode of land transportation. Our commitments is competitive rates and timely delivery of cargo.
